FOOD & ACCOMODATION

<< back

Throughout your stay at Yoga Pathshala the wonderful Niru and team will look after your wellbeing with three nourishing, delicious local meals each day.

Depending on the season, up to 75% of the food is produced at the homestead following organic methods of growing. At meal times you won’t go hungry under Niru’s loving care as you will come to expect her trademark question of ‘More?’. The food at Yoga Pathshala is exceptional and a joy to indulge in. The ayurvedic inspired meals will compliment your journey at Yoga Pathshala. As we know, eating well is another important step in loving yourself and creating a happy and healthy lifestyle. The meals are vegetarian, and of course we cater for any dietary requirements. The following is an example of what to expect day to day:

BREAKFAST

Ayurvedic herbal tea, porridge with dates and raisins or coconut, bananas, papaya or chickpeas. Other breakfast offerings may include samosa, parantha uppama and Nepalese breakfast.


LUNCH & DINNER

Dal (pulses), vegetables (pumpkin, carrot, cauliflower, peas, spinach etc.), rice and chapatti (Roti).

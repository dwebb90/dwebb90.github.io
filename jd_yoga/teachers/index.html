YOGI MASTER RAJAN

Rajan Bastola has more than 20 years experience in the practice of Yoga. He was inspired by the Panini tradition of Yoga and Taoism. In the pursuit of this knowledge, he travelled the Himalayas and the Indian continent, where he developed a strong passion to share his deep understanding of Yoga with thousands of people from all over the world.

Rajan opened the first Yoga Homestay Retreat in Nepal where he instructs daily, imparting his expertise in Hatha Yoga, Pranayama, Meditation, Sound Healing and Reiki. Inspired by this venture, he opened the Yoga Pathsala Teacher Training School so that people from everywhere can learn, develop and share in the benefits of yoga. Rajan is driven to make this YTT school the best in the world! His compassion and good will extends into his community through his facilitation of free yoga clinics in the local communities and Schools of Bhaktapur. He is the Founder and President of the Nepal Red Cross Society, Nagarkot and the President of the local program, Save the Herbs. His philosophy behind his yoga teaching resides in inclusivity, compassion, resilience and peacebuilding and he endeavours to share this with as many people around the globe from his home in Nepal.
